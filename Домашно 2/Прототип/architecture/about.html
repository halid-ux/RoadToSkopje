ABOUT US

Skopje, as the capital and largest city of our country, is the main attraction and tourist magnet for many tourists from all over the world. It abounds with a large number of tourist attractions (monuments, museums), as well as a large number of cafes and restaurants recognizable by our rich cuisine. Whereas every year Skopje is crowded with a huge number of tourists, we decided to design and make a so-called guide in a form of web application that will be of great help to tourists. Our application is genuine a solution for people who travel all the time and cannot have a guide who will guide them shows all famous places, landmarks, bars and so on. Our goal is to make it easier for them users can more easily search and decide which places in Skopje they want to visit, and also to be able to easily find "Rent a car" to get to the desired location faster.
